April 28 · Your Birthday 🌹

Aya,

Let me say this properly. Not because it's your birthday — but because you've been deserving to hear it for a long time now.

My love for you doesn't stay the same. Every time I say "I love you," I mean it more than the time before. I know that sounds like something people just say. But I'm not that kind of person. So every time I tell you again — for the hundredth time, and eventually the millionth — know that each one carries more weight than the last. It grows. Every single day, every hour, without me even trying. That's just what happens when it's real.

✦ ✦ ✦

Your presence alone does something to me that I can't fully explain. You don't even need to say anything — just being near you is enough to change everything. A message from you in the middle of a normal day already turns it into something different. When you come close, I genuinely feel like I could face anything. Every laugh. Every glance. I forget where I am. I would stop time to stay in those moments if I could.

There's one thing I want you to know and I mean this completely: your smile is the one thing I would do anything to protect. Not as a promise I'm making right now — as a decision I already made. You are the most precious person in my life, officially, quietly, completely. You let me be entirely myself without any effort at all. I didn't know how much I needed that kind of ease until I felt it with you.

✦ ✦ ✦

I want to be honest about something. Before you, I was going through days without real direction. After some things that didn't work out, I'd already given up — on relationships, on a lot of things. I was just existing, moving forward without really knowing why. And then you came. You didn't just make things slightly better. You changed the whole picture. You gave meaning to things that had none. You were a light that showed up in the middle of the dark, and I didn't even realize how dark it had gotten until I could see clearly again, with you.

I'm grateful for you in a way that no words were really built for. For every good moment you've given me, for the way you make me feel like myself, for showing up the way you did when you did. Everyone around me sees it. And they're right to.

Happy birthday, Aya.
I love you more than you'll ever fully know. 💕

P.S. — If you're smiling reading this, I did what I set out to do. Now go enjoy your day. You've earned every second of it. 🎂
