April 28 · Your Birthday 🌹

Aya,

Let me say this properly — not because it's your birthday, but because you've deserved to hear it for a long time now.

My love for you doesn't stay the same. Every time I say "I love you," I mean it more than the time before. Every single day, every hour, it grows — without me even trying. That's just what happens when it's real.

✦ ✦ ✦

Your presence alone does something to me I can't fully explain. You don't even need to say anything — just being near you is enough to change everything. When you come close, I feel like I could face anything. Every laugh. Every glance. I forget where I am. I would stop time to stay in those moments if I could.

Your smile is the one thing I would do anything to protect. Not as a promise I'm making right now — as a decision I already made. You let me be entirely myself without any effort at all. I didn't know how much I needed that kind of ease until I felt it with you.

✦ ✦ ✦

Before you, I was just moving through days without real direction. After some things that didn't work out, I had already given up — on relationships, on a lot of things. I was just existing. And then you came. You didn't make things slightly better. You changed the whole picture. You gave meaning to things that had none. You were a light that showed up in the middle of the dark, and I didn't even realize how dark it had gotten until I could see clearly again — with you.

I'm grateful for you in a way no words were really built for. For every good moment you gave me, for the way you make me feel like myself, for showing up the way you did. Everyone around me sees it. And they're right to.

Happy birthday, Aya.
I love you more than you'll ever fully know. 💕

P.S. — If you're smiling reading this, then I did exactly what I set out to do. Now go enjoy your day. You've earned every second of it. 🎂
